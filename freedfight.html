FREEDOM FIGHTERS OF TAMIL NADU

Subramania Bharati (1882-1921): A renowned Tamil poet, journalist, and freedom fighter, Bharati played a vital role in the Indian independence movement. His patriotic poems and writings inspired many people to fight against British colonial rule.

K. Kamarajar (1903-1975): Kamaraj was a freedom fighter and political leader who served as the Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu. He played a crucial role in the Indian National Congress and worked towards education reforms, improving rural development, and women's rights.

Veerapandiya Kattabomman (1760-1799): Kattabomman was a Palayakarrar (polygar) chieftain who fought against British colonial rule in the late 18th century. He resisted the East India Company's oppressive policies and became a symbol of resistance in the region.

Sathyamurthy (1900-1943): Sathyamurthy was a freedom fighter and activist who actively participated in the Indian independence movement. He played a vital role in organizing protests and strikes against the British, advocating for social justice and the rights of workers.

These are just a few examples of the freedom fighters from Tamil Nadu. There are many more individuals and groups who have contributed to the struggle for freedom and social justice in the state.
